Kpop Jimmy

Every walk is a comeback stage.

Official bee file

Vibe Center energy. Humble, and loud about it.
Natural habitat Wherever the lighting reads “stage.” So: everywhere.
Step style Choreographed down to the finger angles.
Honey order One honey shot, “for the vocals.”

The lore

Kpop Jimmy debuted three weeks ago and has already had four comebacks. The blue perm is contractual — nobody has seen the contract, or the agency, or any evidence the agency exists. When asked about the headset mic, which is connected to nothing, he said it’s “for the concept,” bowed exactly 90 degrees, and exited in formation. Alone.

He treats the daily leaderboard like a music show ranking. First place gets a full acceptance speech thanking the hive, the fans, and everyone who believed in him since debut (three weeks ago). Second place gets a gracious smile, one tear held at exactly 80%, and a training montage nobody asked for.

The hive held a vote on his fandom name. He rejected every option and announced the official one himself: Buzznation. Membership is automatic if you’ve ever walked within ten meters of him. Benefits include finger hearts, birthday coverage, and unsolicited vocal warm-ups at dawn.

Bee facts

Fancam physics

Every Bee cam photo of him comes out as a fancam. Perfect angle, slow zoom, dramatic focus. Nobody is filming him like that. It doesn’t matter.

The encore rule

Refuses to stop at 10,000 steps. “The fans want an encore.” The fans are asleep. The encore happens anyway.

The 90-degree bow

Bows to seniors, juniors, vending machines, and one specific tree he believes discovered him.

Vocal rest Wednesdays

Communicates only in gestures every Wednesday to protect the vocals. Still, somehow, the loudest bee in the hive.

Favorite Color Hunt color

Ocean blue

“Official fandom color. I decided this morning.”
