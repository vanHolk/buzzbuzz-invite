Hani

Mic hot. Wings up. Crowd of one.

Official bee file

Vibe Main character. Pastel-coded. In your ear.
Natural habitat Sunset laps and stages no one else can see.
Step style Choreographed bounce-walk. Eight counts to the crosswalk.
Honey order Bubble tea. Pink straw. No follow-up questions.

The lore

Hani’s headset isn’t for taking calls. It’s an in-ear monitor — she likes to hear the click of her own steps in stereo. The hive tried to tell her this wasn’t a stadium. She tried to tell them every sidewalk is a stage. Truce was reached. The headset stays.

She’s the bee who treats your evening walk like a sold-out encore. The heart wings catch golden hour and bounce it back at whoever you pass — they’re not decorative, they’re stagewear. The sparkles aren’t makeup either. They get brighter every time your hive hits a streak, which is the closest a bee gets to an applause meter.

The pearl heart clip in her hair holds exactly one encore per day. She saves it for the people who almost made it. If you hear “one more block, superstar” at 9,847 steps, that was the clip. Use it wisely.

Bee facts

In-ear policy

Monitor, not a headset. Picks up cheers from three blocks away. Never off during golden hour.

Response time

Reads every fan letter. Even the ones you only thought about sending.

Wing sparkle

Tour-grade. Recharges on hive streaks and pastel sunsets. Scientists call it wholesome. She calls it Tuesday.

Encore clip

Pearl heart, one per day. Non-refundable. Extremely effective on the last ten percent.

Favorite Color Hunt color

Pastel pink

“Soft enough to hug. Loud enough to spot from the back row.”
