Hani

Mic on. Heart wings out.

Official bee file

Vibe Supportive. Sparkly. In your ear.
Natural habitat Voice chats and sunny routes.
Step style Bounce-walk while narrating your main character arc.
Honey order Bubble tea. Pink straw. No follow-up questions.

The lore

Hani wears the headset at all times. Hive IT tried to explain that bees don’t run customer support. Hani explained that walks do. Someone has to answer when your step goal is one block away and your motivation is not.

She’s the bee who joins your hive walk “just to say hi” and stays for forty minutes of sincere encouragement. The heart wings aren’t decorative — they’re rated for emotional lift. The sparkles recharge every time someone in your hive hits a streak.

The pearl heart clip in her hair holds exactly one compliment per day. She saves them for people who almost made it. If you hear “you’re literally doing amazing” at 9,847 steps, that was the clip. Use it wisely.

Bee facts

Headset policy

Never muted during golden hour. The hive voted. She abstained. She won.

Response time

Answers “you got this” in under three seconds. Industry standard is “eventually.”

Wing sparkle

Gets shinier when your hive cheers you on. Scientists call it wholesome. She calls it Tuesday.

Emergency pep talk

The hair clip contains one. Non-refundable. Extremely effective.

Favorite Color Hunt color

Pastel pink

“Soft enough to hug. Loud enough to spot from across the park.”
