How BuzzBuzz works

  1. 1. Set your daily step goal

    BuzzBuzz helps you track walking progress without turning your day into a spreadsheet. See your steps, keep your streak alive, and move at your own pace.

  2. 2. Join or invite your hive

    A hive is your small friend network inside BuzzBuzz, designed for lightweight encouragement, walking challenges, and low-pressure social check-ins.

  3. 3. Share one photo a day

    Bee cam diary lets you post one daily photo to your hive. It stays for 24 hours, keeping the feed fresh, real, and not too serious.

  4. 4. Play the Color Hunt

    Each week, BuzzBuzz gives everyone a color prompt. Spot the color on your walk, snap it, and share it before the moment disappears.

BuzzBuzz FAQ

What is BuzzBuzz?

BuzzBuzz is a playful step counter and social walking app. It helps you track steps, connect with friends, join a hive, share one photo a day, and take part in weekly Color Hunt challenges.

Is BuzzBuzz a step counter app?

Yes. BuzzBuzz tracks daily steps and progress, but the experience is designed to feel more social, playful, and low-pressure than a traditional fitness tracker.

Can I use BuzzBuzz with friends?

Yes. BuzzBuzz is built around your hive, a small friend network where you can follow each other's walking progress, share daily moments, and stay motivated together.

What is a hive?

A hive is your friend group inside BuzzBuzz. It is where your steps, daily photos, and Color Hunt moments can be shared with the people you invite.

What is Bee cam diary?

Bee cam diary is the daily photo feature in BuzzBuzz. You can share one real photo a day with your hive, and the photo stays visible for 24 hours.

What is Color Hunt?

Color Hunt is a weekly photo challenge where everyone gets a color prompt. You find that color in the real world, take a picture, and share it with your hive.

Do photos stay forever?

No. Bee cam diary photos are designed to stay visible for 24 hours. The goal is to keep sharing light, fresh, and casual.

Is BuzzBuzz a fitness app or a social app?

BuzzBuzz is both. It is a walking and step tracking app with social features, daily photos, friend hives, and playful challenges.

Do I need to post photos to use BuzzBuzz?

No. You can use BuzzBuzz mainly as a step counter and walking motivation app. Photo sharing and Color Hunt challenges are there when you want to join in.

When will BuzzBuzz be available?

BuzzBuzz is available now on the App Store for iPhone. The Android version on Google Play is coming soon.
